Frequently asked questions

What exactly do I receive?

A high-quality printable PDF delivered instantly after checkout on Etsy. No physical item is shipped — you print it at home as many times as you like.

What ages are the books for?

Our activities are designed for toddlers and preschoolers, roughly ages 2–5. The ABC Activity Book is best suited to ages 3–4.

Can I print more than once?

Yes! One purchase means unlimited prints for your own family — perfect for siblings, do-overs and quiet-time favorites.

Do I need special software or a color printer?

No special software — any device that opens a PDF works. A color printer looks lovely, but the pages are designed to work great in black & white too.

Can I share or resell the files?

The files are for personal, single-family use only. Please don't share, resell or redistribute them — but do tell your friends where to find us. 💛
